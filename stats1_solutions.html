Problem Statement 1:

You survey households in your area to find the average rent they are paying. Find the

standard deviation from the following data:

$1550, $1700, $900, $850, $1000, $950.


ANSWER:

First we need to find the mean, variance and standard deviation,

Mean, X` = 1550+1700+900+850+1000+950 / 6

X` = $ 1158.33


Variance σ² = [ (1158.3-1550)² + (1158.3-1700)² + (1158.3-900)² + (1158.3-850)² + (1158.3-1000)² + (1158.3-950)²] / 6

σ² = 676787 / 6

= 112797.9

Standard deviation σ = √112797.9

σ = 335.85
